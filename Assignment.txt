Silver Badge Assignment

There are 3 use cases to develop. Develop anyone as per your choice, and you must present it in a scheduled session.
Use case 1
Smart Study Buddy 🎓 - An AI tutor that helps you study any subject by creating quizzes, explaining concepts, and tracking your learning progress.
The Problem: Studying i`s boring and ineffective when you’re just re-reading the same material over and over. You need personalized practice and explanations that adapt to your learning style.
Your Solution: Create an AI Application 
•	Reads your textbooks and study materials
•	Generates custom practice questions based on the content
•	Explains difficult concepts in different ways until you understand
•	Tracks what you’ve mastered and what needs more work
•	Creates personalized study schedules
Tech Stack:
•	Content Analysis: For extracting key concepts from study materials
•	Question Generation: Using LLMs to create varied quiz questions
•	Progress Tracking: SQLite database for storing learning data
•	Adaptive Learning: Simple algorithms to adjust difficulty
Advanced Features:
•	Multiple learning styles (visual, auditory, kinesthetic)
•	Integration with popular learning platforms
•	Study group collaboration features
•	Spaced repetition scheduling





Use case 2
Recipe Recommendation Engine 🍳- Generates personalized recipes based on ingredients you have, dietary restrictions, cooking skill level, and taste preferences. Everyone can relate to cooking, making it perfect for demos. Shows practical AI applications that solve daily problems.
The Problem: Meal planning is tedious, and most recipe apps don’t consider what’s actually in your fridge or your cooking abilities.
Your Solution: Create an AI Application that:
•	Suggests recipes based on available ingredients
•	Considers dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
•	Adapts recipes to your cooking skill level
•	Learns from your feedback to improve recommendations
•	Provides nutritional information and cooking tips
Tech Stack:
•	Recipe Database: Web scraping or recipe APIs
•	Ingredient Matching: Text similarity and NLP
•	Personalization: Collaborative filtering algorithms
•	Nutritional Data: Integration with nutrition APIs
Fun Additions:
•	Meal planning for entire weeks
•	Shopping list generation
•	Cooking time optimization
•	Wine and beverage pairings







Use case 3
Local Business Intelligence Assistant 📊- Helps small business owners understand their data through natural language conversations and automated insights.
The Problem: Business analytics tools are complex and expensive for small businesses. Owners need simple ways to understand their sales, customer behavior, and market trends.
Your Solution: Build an AI that:
•	Analyzes sales data, customer feedback, and market trends
•	Answers questions like “Why did sales drop last month?”
•	Generates automated weekly/monthly business reports
•	Identifies opportunities and potential problems
•	Provides actionable recommendations
Tech Stack:
•	Data Processing: Pandas, SQL databases
•	Visualization: Plotly, Matplotlib for charts
•	Business Intelligence: Statistical analysis libraries
•	Dashboard: Streamlit or custom web interface
Advanced Features:
•	Competitor analysis
•	Customer sentiment tracking
•	Inventory management insights
•	Marketing campaign optimization
